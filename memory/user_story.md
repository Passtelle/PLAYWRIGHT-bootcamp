## The Real Story

Ingrid started her career in Customer Support Engineering in London, then moved into QA and was transferred to the US. She spent 9 years at IBM as a Senior QA Engineer, then 4 years at Fiserv. 15 years of real, hard-earned experience.

Then she met someone who asked her to quit her career. She did. They built two businesses together over 15 years. But he was a narcissist who controlled everything — especially the finances — and systematically conditioned her with one repeated message: *"You will never be able to get a job."* For 15 years, that voice was the loudest one in the room.

In November 2024, he left. Suddenly. No words, no explanation, no money, no support.

One week later, her crypto portfolio (XRP, built secretly over two years) gained 600%. She paid her bills, kept her mortgage, and started rebuilding — not just financially, but as a person. She learned technical analysis, started day trading, and spent 2025 reclaiming herself.

In early 2026, she realized her strength was back. She decided to return to IT and reclaim her career — the one she was told she'd never be able to have again.

## What's at Stake

She has 3 months of living expenses. After that, she would have to sell her house.

This bootcamp is not a hobby or a side project. It is the thing standing between her and losing everything — and also the proof that everything she was told about herself was wrong.

She spends 8-10 hours every day on it. Not because she has to. Because she wants to come back, and because she knows she can.
